Who requires to report?

Regulation of the Minister of Health of the Republic of Indonesia No.58 of 2016 applies to both Medical device and Pharmaceutical companies.



Local Authority or Association

Corruption Eradication Commission (known as Komisi Pemberantasan Korupsi or KPK). Click here to visit the official website.



Reporting Due Date

30 working days after providing sponsorship.



Threshold Amount

None


Who are reportable as Recipients?

Healthcare Professional

Healthcare professional as defined by the Republic of Indonesia No.58 of 2016, either government employees, non-government employees or individual medical practitioners.

Healthcare Organization

Healthcare Organization as defined by the Republic of Indonesia No.58 of 2016, Healthcare insitutions, health service facility Organizations or professional Organizations.


Reporting Scope

Republic of Indonesia No.58 of 2016 requires to report sponsorship and related support.
