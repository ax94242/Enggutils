Who requires to report?

Pharmaceutical company payments and disclosure initaitive from SFDA applies to both Medical device and Pharmaceutical companies.



Local Authority or Association

Saudi Food and drug authority (SFDA). Click here to visit the official website.



Reporting Due Date

Quarterly: Within 10 working days after Quarter close.



Threshold Amount

Above 50 SAR per transaction or above 500 SAR in total per year with respect to a recipient.


Who are reportable as Recipients?

Healthcare Professional

Healthcare professional as defined by the Pharmaceutical company payments and disclosure initiative from SFDA: All healthcare related individual licensed to practice in Saudi Arabia with Saudi Arabia citizenship.

Healthcare Organization

Healthcare Organization as defined by the Pharmaceutical company payments and disclosure initiative from SFDA: All healthcare related entities registered and located in Saudi Arabia.


Reporting Scope

Pharmaceutical company payments and disclosure initiative has a wide definition of reportable requirement and does not limit it. As stated by the regulation document, Companies must report all financial support provided to the recipients Â but not limited to:
1. Consulting fees.
2. Speaker fees.
3. Training fees.
4. Sponsorship to educational event, symposium or conference.
5. Research or educational grants (restricted or unrestricted)
6. Hospitality.
7. Educational materials.
