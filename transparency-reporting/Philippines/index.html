Who requires to report?

Administrative order No. 2015/0053 applies to both Medical device and Pharmaceutical companies.



Local Authority or Association

Food and Drug Administration Philippines. Click here to visit the official website.



Reporting Due Date

30 days prior to the event occurence or throughout the calendar year for post travel.



Threshold Amount

None


Who are reportable as Recipients?

Healthcare Professional

Healthcare professional as defined by the Administrative Order No. 2015/0053, Doctors with active practicing license in Philippines and ability to prescribe medicine to patients. Defined interaction (Reporting Scope section below) of the companies with HCP are required to report.

Healthcare Organization

No reporting scope for Healthcare organization as per Administrative Order No. 2015/0053.


Reporting Scope

Administrative Order No. 2015/0053 requires to report two types of interaction with an Healthcare Professional (HCP):

1. Notice of Meetings: Notice of Meetings is a pre-activity information on the Meeting or Symposia or any associated sponsorship. If more than 100 HCPs will be attending an face to face event, then companies are required to file the report with the government agency i.e. Philippines FDA, 30 days prior to the date of event.

2. Post Travel: Post Travel report must be submitted to the Philippines FDAâ€™s website whenever the companies sponsored an Healthcare Professional (HCP) to travel to an event for continued medical education. Continued medical education can include trainings, congresses, events etc.

Updates

Release of Philippines New Administrative Order is under progress. Scope and defintion of transparency reporting of the current draft version is not much clear. Waiting for the new draft version.
Key Takeways according to the draft version:
1. New due dates: Monthly reporting deadlines.
2. Publishing reports in the new Philippines FDA Portal and publishing in companies own external website.
3. Penalty Clause is not clear. No guidelines for no reporting declaration from companies.
